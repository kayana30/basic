About us

Dr.Nissanka Wijeyeratne, the Cabinet Minister of Education and Higher Education at the time, came up with the idea for the Open University of Sri Lanka. The OUSL has the same legal and academic standing as any other Sri Lankan national university. At the OUSL, distance education is delivered through a dynamic network of regional and study centers, with knowledge delivered through a variety of media. As a result, students with employment obligations can continue their studies since they interact with specially created self-instructional materials that act as a "Tutor in Print." This strategy gives the finest potential learning opportunity for distance learners, allowing them to balance personal, work-related, and academic obligations.
